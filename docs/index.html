The Problem

Insurance fraud is a $80 billion annual crisis.

Traditional fraud detection relies on manual review — slow, inconsistent, impossible to scale. InsureIQ was built to change that with AI.

🧠
The AI Brain
Claude AI reads every claim the way a senior analyst would — extracting claimant details, incident type, amount, and red flags. No forms, no templates. Pure intelligence applied to raw text.
🔍
The Memory Layer
Every claim is instantly compared against 500+ historical cases stored in ChromaDB — like giving the AI years of institutional experience in milliseconds.
⚡
The Risk Engine
An XGBoost ML model scores each claim's fraud probability in real time. High risk triggers investigation. Low risk moves to fast approval. Clear decisions every time.
📊
The Executive Dashboard
Every claim flows into Snowflake's three-layer data warehouse (RAW → TRANSFORM → MART) and surfaces in a custom-built HTML executive dashboard showing fraud rates, risk distribution, and claim volume in real time.
